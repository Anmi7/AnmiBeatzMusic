About Me

Anmi Beatz, whose real name is Jake Baranda, is a Filipino music producer known for his work in hip-hop, rap, and R&B. He has built a reputation for creating high-quality instrumentals that blend smooth melodies with hard-hitting drums, making his beats popular among independent artists and rappers.

His production style is influenced by modern trends, incorporating atmospheric sounds and rhythmic elements that enhance the energy of a track.
